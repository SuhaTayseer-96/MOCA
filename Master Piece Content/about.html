Our Mission

At Faid, our mission is to reduce food waste and support communities in need. Every day, countless amounts of food go to waste while many people face hunger. We connect surplus food from restaurants to local charities to ensure it reaches those who need it the most.

How We Work

  • Food Collection: Restaurants notify us when they have surplus food available for donation.
  • Logistics & Delivery: Our team coordinates pickups and ensures safe delivery to trusted charities.
  • Community Support: The donated food is distributed by our charity partners, reaching those in need.

Our Impact

500,000+ Meals Donated: Since our launch, we've helped deliver over 500,000 meals to communities in need.
100+ Restaurant Partners: We collaborate with local restaurants to minimize food waste.
Trusted by Charities: Our charity partners rely on us to provide a steady supply of surplus food.

Why We Do It

Every year, millions of tons of food go to waste while many individuals face hunger. We believe that, by working together, we can reduce waste and make a real impact. Our goal is to create a more sustainable future by ensuring that no good food goes unused.

Our Partners

We work with a variety of restaurants and food vendors who are committed to sustainability and reducing waste. Through our collaboration with reputable charities, we ensure that every meal donated makes a real difference in people's lives.
